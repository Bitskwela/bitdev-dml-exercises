## Theory & Lecture Content

AI is commonly categorized into three levels based on capability. Understanding these levels is critical to separating reality from science fiction.

### 1. Narrow AI (Artificial Narrow Intelligence / ANI)

**What it is:** AI that is designed and trained for ONE specific task. It is extremely good at that one thing — often better than humans — but it cannot do anything else.

**Status:** This is the ONLY type of AI that exists today.

**Analogy:** Think of a specialist doctor. An ophthalmologist is world-class at treating eye problems but cannot perform heart surgery. Narrow AI is the same — brilliant at its specialty, useless at everything else.

**Real-world examples:**

| Narrow AI System | Its ONE Job | What It CANNOT Do |
|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Grab routing AI | Find the fastest route for your driver | Recommend what restaurant to eat at |
| GCash fraud detection | Spot suspicious transactions | Translate Filipino to English |
| Shopee recommendations | Suggest products you might buy | Write your homework essay |
| Siri / Google Assistant | Answer voice commands | Drive a car |
| Netflix recommendations | Suggest shows you might like | Detect cancer in X-rays |
| Spam filter | Catch junk emails | Compose a love letter |
| ChatGPT | Generate text responses | Physically cook you adobo |
| Google Translate | Translate between languages | Recognize your face |
| Face Unlock | Recognize your face | Translate a document |
| Waze | Predict traffic and find routes | Recommend a movie |

Notice the pattern? Each system does ONE thing. None of them can do what the others do.

### 2. General AI (Artificial General Intelligence / AGI)

**What it is:** A hypothetical AI that has human-level intelligence — it can learn any task, reason about any topic, adapt to any situation, just like a human can.

**Status:** DOES NOT EXIST. No one has built this. No one knows how to build this. Many researchers believe we are decades away, if it is even possible at all.

**Analogy:** Think of a general practitioner doctor who is also a lawyer, engineer, chef, artist, and musician — all at human level. That is how hard AGI is to build.

**What it would need to do:**
- Learn new tasks without being specifically trained for them
- Transfer knowledge between completely different domains
- Have common sense reasoning
- Understand context, emotion, and nuance like a human
- Be creative, not just responsive

**Why it is so hard:** Current AI learns patterns from data for specific tasks. Making a system that truly *understands* the world the way humans do is an unsolved problem in computer science and neuroscience.

### 3. Super AI (Artificial Super Intelligence / ASI)

**What it is:** A hypothetical AI that surpasses human intelligence in EVERY field — science, creativity, social skills, problem-solving, everything.

**Status:** PURE SCIENCE FICTION. If we cannot even build General AI, Super AI is orders of magnitude further away.

**Analogy:** This is the Terminator, Ultron, Skynet. The stuff of movies. Entertaining to think about, but not something to lose sleep over.

### The Comparison Table

| Feature | Narrow AI (ANI) | General AI (AGI) | Super AI (ASI) |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Exists today?** | YES | NO | NO |
| **Can do** | One specific task | Any task a human can | Everything, better than all humans |
| **Example** | Spam filter, Grab, Siri | (Does not exist) | (Science fiction) |
| **Analogy** | Specialist doctor | Human-level thinker | Superhuman genius |
| **Should you worry?** | No — it is a tool | Maybe someday | Not in our lifetime |
| **Philippine example** | GCash fraud detection | Theoretical | Terminator / Ultron |

### The Key Insight

**Every single AI system you use today is Narrow AI.** When someone says "AI will take over the world," they are talking about General or Super AI — which do not exist and may never exist.

This does not mean Narrow AI is not powerful. Narrow AI can beat the world champion at chess, translate documents in milliseconds, detect cancer better than some doctors, and drive cars. But it can only do the one thing it was built for.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Narrow AI (ANI)** is the ONLY type of AI that exists today. It can do one specific task very well but nothing else.
2. **General AI (AGI)** would have human-level intelligence across all domains. It does NOT exist and no one knows how to build it.
3. **Super AI (ASI)** would surpass all human intelligence. It is pure science fiction.
4. **All AI you interact with daily is Narrow AI** — Grab, GCash, Shopee, Siri, ChatGPT, Netflix, Waze, face unlock, spam filters.
5. **Do not fear AI — learn to build it.** Understanding Narrow AI is where all the real opportunities are: jobs, startups, hackathons, and solving problems for Filipino communities.
