## Lesson 25 Exercises: ER Diagram Practice

---
### 1. Identify Entities (7 minutes)
Scenario: Library system tracks books, authors, members, loans. List entities.

---
### 2. Define Attributes (8 minutes)
For Book entity, list 5 attributes (id, title, ...). Identify primary key.

---
### 3. One-to-Many Relationship (8 minutes)
Draw: Author → Books (one author, many books). Label foreign key.

---
### 4. One-to-One Relationship (7 minutes)
Resident ↔ Profile (one resident, one profile). Draw and label keys.

---
### 5. Many-to-Many with Junction (10 minutes)
Students ↔ Clubs. Design Memberships junction table (student_id, club_id). Draw diagram.

---
### 6. Foreign Key Identification (7 minutes)
Given: Orders(id, customer_id), Customers(id, name). Identify foreign key.

---
### 7. Cardinality Notation (7 minutes)
Label relationships: 1:1, 1:N, N:M for three examples.

---
### 8. Stretch: Full Scholarship System ER (Optional)
Entities: Resident, Application, ScholarshipProgram, Approval. Draw full ER with relationships.

---
### 9. Reflection (3 minutes)
Two mistakes prevented by upfront ER design.
