# Quiz 1
## Scenario: Applicant Age Analysis
Tian summarizes age distribution.

**Question 1:** Mean calculates:
A. Middle value
B. Most frequent
C. Arithmetic average
D. Range

**Answer:** C  
**Explanation:** Mean sums and divides by count.

---

**Question 2:** Median advantage over mean:
A. Faster computation
B. Robust to outliers
C. Always larger
D. Requires sorting never

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Median unaffected by extreme values.

---

**Question 3:** Standard deviation measures:
A. Central value
B. Spread around mean
C. Maximum value
D. Mode frequency

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Std dev quantifies variability.

---

**Question 4:** Q1, Q2, Q3 represent:
A. Top 3 values
B. 25th, 50th, 75th percentiles
C. Mean variants
D. Error margins

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Quartiles split data into four equal parts.

---

**Question 5:** Outlier detection uses:
A. Mean only
B. IQR fences
C. Mode count
D. Alphabet order

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** IQR * 1.5 rule identifies outliers.

# Quiz 2
## Scenario: Probability Estimates
Rhea Joy models approval likelihood.

**Question 6:** P(event) formula:
A. favorable / total
B. total / favorable
C. favorable * total
D. total - favorable

**Answer:** A  
**Explanation:** Probability = favorable outcomes / total.

---

**Question 7:** Independent events A, B: P(A and B) =
A. P(A) + P(B)
B. P(A) * P(B)
C. P(A) - P(B)
D. P(A) / P(B)

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Independent events multiply probabilities.

---

**Question 8:** Conditional probability P(A|B):
A. P(A) always
B. P(A and B) / P(B)
C. P(B) / P(A)
D. P(A) + P(B)

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Conditional: restrict to B occurring.

---

**Question 9:** Z-score measures:
A. Absolute value
B. Std devs from mean
C. Median distance
D. Mode count

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Z-score standardizes position in distribution.

---

**Question 10:** Normal distribution shape:
A. Flat line
B. Bell curve
C. Exponential decay
D. Step function

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Normal curve symmetric bell shape.
